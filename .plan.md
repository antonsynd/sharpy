**Precedence levels to verify (from spec)**:
1. `()`, `[]`, `.`, `?.` - Highest (primary)
2. `**` - Right-associative
3. `+x`, `-x`, `~x` - Unary
4. `*`, `/`, `//`, `%` - Multiplicative
5. `+`, `-` - Additive
6. `<<`, `>>` - Shifts
7-9. `&`, `^`, `|` - Bitwise
10. `|>` - Pipe
11. `to` - Type coercion
12. `==`, `!=`, `<`, `<=`, `>`, `>=`, `in`, `is` - Comparisons (chained)
13. `not` - Logical NOT
14. `and` - Logical AND
15. `or` - Logical OR
16. `??` - Null coalesce
